How to Borrow Digital Books from e-Book Collection (EBSCO)

In order to download EBSCO eBooks to your computer or your portable device to read with the Adobe Digital Editions, you must create a personal My EBSCOhost folder.

Instructions

  1. Select the eBook Collection (EBSCO) from the A to Z Databases.
  2. Search EBSCOhost for eBook titles of interest.
  3. Select the book that you would like to read and click on “Full Download.”
  4. Check the checkbox for Adobe Digital Editions.
  5. Click on the "Full Download" button.
  6. Enjoy reading your book on your computer or transfer to your preferred e-reader device.
