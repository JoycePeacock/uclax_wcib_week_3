How to Borrow Digital Books from e-Book Collection (EBSCO)

You can get more great titles by downloading ebooks from our EBSCO collection. They are easy to use and can be transferred to your favorite portable e-reader.

Instructions

Step 1

Select the eBook Collection (EBSCO) from the A to Z Databases.

Step 2

Search EBSCOhost for eBook titles of interest.

Note: You can find books by performing a search or by browsing the collection.

Step 3

Select the book that you would like to read and click on “Full Download.”

Note: In order to download EBSCO eBooks to your computer or your portable device to read with the Adobe Digital Editions, you must create a personal My EBSCOhost folder.

Step 4

Check the checkbox for Adobe Digital Editions and click on the "Full Download" button.

Step 5

Enjoy reading your book on your computer or transfer to your preferred e-reader device.
